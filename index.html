Nick Henning

The guitar is my passion and the heart of everything I do as a musician. For over a decade, I’ve been writing songs, crafting riffs, and pouring emotion and energy into my music. Vocals play just as big a role—whether raw and powerful, melancholic, or straight to the point. Stylistically, I move between pop-punk and alternative rock—somewhere between driving, atmospheric, and a little nostalgic.
